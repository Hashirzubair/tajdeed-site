FAQ

Which documents can I track?

Residence visa, Emirates ID, passport, driving license, vehicle registration (Mulkiya), car insurance, health insurance, Ejari tenancy and labour card — each with UAE-specific renewal notes and official portal links.

Are the fine amounts official?

They are estimates based on published UAE rules (e.g. AED 20/day after the Emirates ID grace period, capped at AED 1,000). Always verify with official channels — Tajdeed links you straight to them.

Do I need an account?

No. Tajdeed has no accounts at all. Your data lives only on your phone — we couldn't see it even if we wanted to.

Is Tajdeed a government app?

No. Tajdeed is an independent app and is not affiliated with any UAE government entity. It is informational only.

How much does it cost?

Tajdeed is free. Everything you see today stays free. Later we may add a one-time Pro unlock for unlimited documents and family members — a single small payment, never a subscription.
