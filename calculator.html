How UAE overstay fines work

Residence and visit visas

Since October 2022, the UAE applies a unified overstay fine of AED 50 per day. This calculator assumes fines from the day after expiry (the worst case). Some visa types and statuses have grace periods before fines begin — always confirm your own case with ICP.

Emirates ID

You have 30 days after expiry to renew. After that, the fine is AED 20 per day, capped at AED 1,000.

Good to know

  • Overstay fines must be settled before leaving the country.
  • Emirates ID renewal is tied to your residence visa — renew the visa first.
  • Most visit visas can be extended from inside the UAE if you apply before expiry.
