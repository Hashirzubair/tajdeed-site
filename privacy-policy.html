Tajdeed (تجديد) — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 3 July 2026

The short version

Your data never leaves your device. Tajdeed collects nothing, transmits nothing, and has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

What the app stores

Tajdeed stores the document types you track, the names you assign to them (e.g. "You", a family member's first name), expiry dates, and your reminder preferences. This information is saved in a local database on your device only. Tajdeed never asks for and cannot store document numbers, photos, or scans.

What the app does NOT do

  • No data is collected, transmitted, or sold — to us or anyone else.
  • No user accounts, sign-ins, or cloud sync.
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs.
  • No access to your contacts, photos, camera, or location.

Notifications

Reminders are local notifications, scheduled entirely on your device. No notification content is ever sent to a server.

External links

The app links to official renewal portals (for example icp.gov.ae or rta.ae). These open in your browser and are governed by those sites' own privacy policies.

Deleting your data

Deleting a document removes it permanently. Uninstalling the app removes all app data from your device. There is nothing to delete anywhere else, because nothing ever leaves your device.

Disclaimer

Tajdeed is not affiliated with any UAE government entity. All fine estimates and renewal information are informational only — always verify with official channels.
